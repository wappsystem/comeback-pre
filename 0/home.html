The Book:
The Comeback Method – A Practical Guide To Finding Your Path And Staying On It



The Comeback Method details how you can get back up again and succeed at anything you set your mind to. This book is your guide to finding your way back to yourself so that you can experience success, happiness and a sense of fulfillment every day. You can apply The Comeback Method to every aspect of your life, from work to play. Use it with your family, your friends, even with your colleagues. This is your time to succeed! This is your comeback!

The Author:
Patric J. Jansson, PhD, MSc to inner motivation, relaxed focus and everyday endurance.

The Comeback Method is based on the lessons Dr. Patric J. Jansson learned as a professional tennis player in Scandinavia, his studies in philosophy and psychology and his work as a cancer researcher on how stress affects humans.

Chapter One:
The Big Picture – It’s All Connected

This initial chapter will provide the big picture of how everything else in this book is connected. Through a scientific lens, we’ll explore how your brain and body are connected, too, and how we interact with the outside world. Gaining a greater knowledge of how your brain and body work will deepen your understanding of why you feel the way you feel and act the way you act…and therefore get the results you tend to get. In later chapters, you’ll learn how to master your neurotransmitters by taking meaningful actions that align and work in a way that’s right for you.

Chapter Two:
The Event – Your Turning Point

This chapter will help you recognize the events that caused you to question everything. These highly stressful situations can either put you in a downward spiral or function as a springboard for your comeback and success.

Chapter Three:
The Guiding Star – The Gift that Guides Your Every Action

We explore how you can find your gift so that you can work in your element. You can use your Guiding Star in all situations you encounter. You’ll feel happy when you use it, because that’s when you can feel like yourself and at your best. You’ll create your personal Guiding Star Statement, which includes instructions for how you can use your gift every day.

Chapter Four:
The Purpose – Your Ultimate Goal

In this chapter, you’ll define your life’s purpose, which may have eluded you before. The purpose you’ll find is already there, rooted in your deepest emotional experiences. You’ll also learn how you can fulfill your Purpose by using the gifts you discovered in the previous chapter as part of your Guiding Star. This chapter will instill a stronger sense of meaning in your life, starting with what it means to feel successful on the inside. You’ll create your own Purpose Statement and will gain the knowledge you need to take action on what you really want.

Chapter Five:
The Plan – Your Plan of Action

We’ll discuss what success means and is for you. We set Big Goals and find the things you really want but never thought you could achieve (or the things you never allowed yourself to want to begin with). We remove things that hold you back and break down your Big Goals into achievable steps that you can take every day. We set up and organize your day so that you’ll always be and feel successful, and we’ll also sprinkle in some challenges that you can use to boost your motivation. You’ll learn how to use the Master, Success and Challenge Lists to remove your stress and experience success every day. With each successful step, this will build your confidence that you can do anything you put your mind to. You’ll create your Big Plan and you’ll have the tools to take action on it every day. Chapter Six:
The Driver – Drive Your Life

We’ll look at what you need to do to become the Driver of your life. This chapter breaks down when you’re driving with intention and also reveals when you think and feel you’re driving but you really aren’t. This chapter also helps you become aware of your driving habits and how to start driving through life with ease.

Chapter Seven:
The Passenger – The Roadblocks on Your Journey

In this chapter, we discover the roadblocks you’ve inadvertently put out for yourself, and you’ll learn how to recognize what’s happening in your life through the different “Passengers” who get onto your bus. You’ll learn how to immediately recognize them by understanding how their presence makes you feel and the ways in which they stop you from experiencing success and enjoying life. We look at the triggers that invite the Passengers and how they kick you out of the driver’s seat. We also look at three unique comeback methods that put you back into the driver’s seat, supporting you to go wherever you want in life. If you master any of these three unique approaches to making a comeback, anything is possible! Knowing how to remove (or drive around) any obstacles that might suddenly appear before you is the most important skill you’ll ever learn in life. When you do that, you’re unlocking the power within!

Chapter Eight:
The Checklist – The Tools You Need to Make Your Comeback

By this point, you will have read the book, but nothing will happen if you don’t take action on your knowledge. The Checklist chapter provides short summaries to help you revisit the book as needed, and it will remind you of the key concepts and tools you can use to make your comeback.
